operations

Play a major role in the Sophomore Year & help run day to day operations.

Director of Academics

Responsibilities

  • Maintain & update the Study Resources Portion of the Stuyvesant Sophomore Caucus.
  • Create & direct the creation of Study Guides for Finals.
  • Take initiative to help improve the education for all Stuyvesant Sophomores.

Advisory Council Chair

Responsibilities

  • The Advisory Council Chair will be elected by the Advisory Council at the first meeting of the year.
  • They are to affrim that the Secretary is fullfilling his/her duties in terms of taking minutes, as well as publishing them.
  • In the absense of the President or Vice President, they are to hold up the agenda and vote on major issues.

Wild Card

Do you want to make a difference at Stuy? If you know what you want to do, and you know how to do it, you can lead your own initiative with us. Or if you know of a way you can help us, you can now create your own position. Simply apply, and we'll work it out.
